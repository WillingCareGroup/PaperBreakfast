# Research Interest Profile

## Primary Focus Areas

### 1. Hematopoietic Stem and Progenitor Cells (HSC / HSPC)
- Hematopoietic stem cell (HSC) and hematopoietic stem and progenitor cell (HSPC) biology
- Ex vivo expansion of HSCs and HSPCs
- Stem cell niche, self-renewal, and engraftment mechanisms
- Stem cell mobilization (G-CSF, plerixafor, CXCR4/SDF-1 axis)
- Single-cell multi-omics of hematopoietic hierarchies
- Umbilical cord blood expansion and clinical translation

### 2. Cell Therapy Engineering and Manufacturing
- GMP-compatible manufacturing processes for cell therapies
- Lentiviral, retroviral, and AAV vector development and optimization
- CRISPR/Cas9, base editing, and prime editing in primary hematopoietic cells
- Ex vivo gene correction and gene addition strategies
- CAR construct design, optimization, and new target discovery
- Scalable manufacturing and cost reduction for cell therapies
- Cryopreservation and product stability

### 3. Clinical Cell Therapy
- CAR-T cell therapies (new antigens, new indications, resistance mechanisms, toxicity management)
- CAR-NK and CAR-macrophage approaches
- Allogeneic / off-the-shelf cell therapy products
- iPSC-derived immune and hematopoietic cell therapies
- Hematopoietic stem cell transplantation (HSCT) outcomes and innovations
- Clinical trials for hematologic malignancies (AML, ALL, MDS, CLL, lymphoma)
- Gene therapy for hemoglobinopathies (sickle cell disease, beta-thalassemia)
- Regulatory and translational milestones in cell and gene therapy

### 4. AI / ML in Protein and Cell Engineering
- De novo protein design (RFdiffusion, ProteinMPNN, AlphaFold, ESMFold)
- ML-guided directed evolution and protein optimization
- Foundation models for biology and drug discovery
- AI-assisted CAR design and T-cell receptor engineering
- Computational prediction of cell therapy outcomes

### 5. Major Breakthroughs (Any Field)
- Nature / Cell / Science tier findings with clear biomedical significance
- First-in-human gene or cell therapy results with strong outcomes
- New treatment paradigms with near-term clinical translation potential
- Landmark platform technologies applicable across cell therapy

---

## Lower Priority (score conservatively)
- Purely observational or epidemiological clinical studies with no mechanistic insight
- Cardiovascular, neurological, or metabolic disease papers unless directly involving stem cell or gene therapy
- Drug discovery for small molecules unless they target HSC biology or enhance cell therapy
- Agricultural, environmental, or microbial biology
- Basic biochemistry studies without clear translational relevance
